Yahoo Finance provides free access to historical stock prices, real-time data, financial statements, and more. It's widely used for training machine learning models in stock price prediction, trend analysis, and portfolio optimization. Python libraries like yfinance make it easy to pull structured financial datasets for training AI models.Quandl offers premium and free datasets for economic indicators, stock market data, commodities, and alternative finance. It’s useful for building predictive models for macroeconomic forecasting, asset pricing, and financial risk modeling.
